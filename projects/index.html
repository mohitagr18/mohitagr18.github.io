Exploring Healthcare Provider Shortage in America

America has a severe shortage of healthcare providers. Demand for services is increasing and shortages of physicians and mental health providers are limiting patients’ access to required treatment.

In this 3 part study, we use the National Plan and Provider Enumeration System (NPPES) healthcare provider data to:

  • Identify shortage areas for physicians and mental healthcare providers in the U.S.
  • Identify sociodemographic and economic factors that influence access to providers.

  • Identify new locations for providers to setup offices in shortage areas.
